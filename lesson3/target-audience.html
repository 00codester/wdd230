Target Audience

The target audience for this site is primarily small business owners in the Ogden UT area. Age is not really important.

Personas

Emily is a 24 year old mother of 2 young children and owns an online clothing sales business. She is looking on the site for events where she can display and promote some of her clothing in order to bring new local customers to her site.

Reid is a 40 single father who owns a gymnastics and parkour gym. He is searching for other local businesses that can supply equipment for his gym.

Scenarios

Are there other local small business owners struggling to find clients?

Can I meet other like-minded individuals?

Are there any job fairs coming up where I could hire more people?

Is ogden the best place to conduct my business?

What are some things to do in Ogden on the weekend?
