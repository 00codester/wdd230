Coastal Info

The entire California coast stretches for 840 miles passing major cities such as San Francisco, San Diego, and Los Angeles. Many majestic and picturesque views await those that venture any length of the coastline. While beautiful, the water along the coast is colder than one is led to believe. For this reason many people don't venture too far into the water. Though swimming in the brisk water is not done for long, surfing is a major pasttime along the coast.
