Bountiful Foods

Our History:

Our company was founded in 2001. Our founder Cody Karl was an avid explorer of the coast of California. During his treck he would often enjoy the locally grown fruit. He fell in love with the fruit and decided to open up Bountiful Foods with the goal of sharing his findings with everyone. Our First and home store is located in Carlsbad California. We have fruit and drink bars all along the coast from San Diego to Los Angeles. Cody wanted to ensure the best quality of fruits for the customers. To do so he only uses locally grown organic fruits.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to provide locals and visiting tourists with a Healthy, Organic, and Convenient refreshment.
